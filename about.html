About Spinfinity

Spinfinity was born from a simple idea — loop your favorite YouTube videos *forever*, without the noise, ads, or complications.

Whether you're looping your favorite meditation music, an affirmation, study vibes, or just that one song that hits different — Spinfinity is here to make it seamless.

✨ What Makes Us Different?

  • No ads
  • No logins
  • No tracking
  • Simple, fast, and minimal

👩‍💻 Who Built This?

This tool was built independently — not by a company, but by a creator who wanted a more peaceful internet. It's a love letter to simplicity and digital freedom.

🌎 Our Mission

We want to create small, beautiful tools that people actually need — tools that bring joy, calm, and control back into your digital life. Spinfinity is just the beginning.
